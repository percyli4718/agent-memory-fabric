# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Agent Memory Fabric to be a respectful, technically serious, and welcoming project for contributors
across different backgrounds, geographies, and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent while giving clear technical feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and design decisions rather than people
- help maintain a safe and inclusive collaboration environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- doxxing, intimidation, or threats
- bad-faith disruption of project discussions or review processes

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying project standards, reviewing contributions fairly, and intervening
when behavior harms the health of the community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through the private contact path
documented in `SECURITY.md` or the repository support channels once established.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.
